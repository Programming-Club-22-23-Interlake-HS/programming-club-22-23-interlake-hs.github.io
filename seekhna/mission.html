Our Mission

Make studying and learning more accesible, better and cheaper for everyone around the world.

The History of Seekhna

Founded in a classroom filled with high school students, Seekhna was founded to replace the traditional methods of studying. Seekhna's goal is to make studying more fun, interactive and useful, as we faced problems ourselves figuring out ways to study. That's why Seekhna is made by Students for Students.
